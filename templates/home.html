Besides from using our built in address subdomain.m.ly, you can also map your own domain name for free.

For example, if you want to set mc.example.com as your server address, simply add a CNAME record mapped to your-Minecraft-UUID.m.ly

Our Technology

The challenge is to handle situations when thousands of players will gather in tight spaces, like in a world, or a chunk and to do this without interrupting the gameplay.

To handle potentially millions of players simultaneously online at the same time, we have developed our own technology. Server nodes are assigned to regions that are more densely populated, and these change the frequency of updates of remote entities to maintain a fluid experience for nearby entities.
