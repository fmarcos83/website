Custom Domain

Besides from using our built in address subdomain.m.ly, you can also map your own domain name for free.

For example, if you want to set mc.example.com as your server address, simply add a CNAME record mapped to your-Minecraft-UUID.m.ly

Our Technology

The biggest challenge in MMO games is to handle situations when a lot of players gather in tight spaces, like in a world, or a spawn and to do this without interrupting the gameplay.

To handle that, we have developed our own technology. More server resources are assigned to worlds that are more densely populated, and the live tweaking system changes the frequency of updates of entities to maintain a fluid experience for all players.
