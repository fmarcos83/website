Our Technology

The biggest challenge in MMO games is to handle situations when a lot of players gather in tight spaces, like in a world, or a spawn and to do this without interrupting the gameplay.

To handle that, we have developed our own technology, that can scale up to a few thousands of servers and petabytes of hot storage. More server resources are assigned to chunks that are more densely populated, and the live-tweak system changes the frequency of updates of entities to maintain a fluid experience for all players.
